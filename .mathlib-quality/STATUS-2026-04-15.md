## What's Blocking the Build (55 errors)

### Cluster A: T_gen_algebraicIndependent (~28 errors, lines 4877-4995)
**Root cause**: Missing lemma `HeckeRing.GLn.Inj.monomial_eval_kronecker`.

Required signature:
```lean
private lemma monomial_eval_kronecker (p : ℕ) (hp : p.Prime)
    (a₁ b₁ a₂ b₂ : ℕ) (h : b₂ ≤ b₁) :
    (T_gen 2 p 0 ^ a₁ * T_gen 2 p 1 ^ b₁)
        (T_diag (ppowDiag 2 p ![b₂, a₂ + b₂])) =
    if a₁ = a₂ ∧ b₁ = b₂ then 1 else 0
```

**Math (Shimura Th 3.24, n=2)**: Using equation (5)
`T(p)·T(1, p^k) = T(1, p^(k+1)) + (p+1 if k=1 else p)·T(p, p^k)`,
the basis cosets `T(p^k, p^l)` (k≤l) factor as `T(1, p^{l-k}) · T(p,p)^k`.
Multiplying out `T(1,p)^a₁ · T(p,p)^b₁` by induction gives a unique leading
term at `T(p^{b₁}, p^{a₁+b₁})` with coefficient 1; non-matching cosets get 0.

**Existing infrastructure (READY TO USE)**:
- `LeanModularForms/HeckeRIngs/GL2/MultiplicationTable.lean:487`:
  `T_sum_prime_mul_T_ad` — exactly Shimura 3.24 (5) at GL level, FULLY PROVED
- `LeanModularForms/HeckeRIngs/GL2/MultiplicationTable.lean:627`:
  `T_sum_ppow_recurrence` — Shimura 3.24 (6), FULLY PROVED
- `LeanModularForms/HeckeRIngs/GL2/MultiplicationTable.lean:94`:
  `T_ad_one_ppow_eq` — `T_ad 1 (p^k) = T_sum (p^k) - T_pp p · T_sum (p^{k-2})`
- `LeanModularForms/HeckeRIngs/GL2/Basic.lean:269`:
  `T_elem_mul_scalar` — scalar shift identity
- `LeanModularForms/HeckeRIngs/GL2/Basic.lean:294`:
